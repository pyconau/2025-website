# PyCon AU, Australia's peak Python conference

In 2025, PyCon AU was held from **Friday the 12th to Tuesday the 16th of September** at **Pullman Melbourne On The Park** in Narrm/Melbourne.

Keynotes from [Dr. Arwen Griffioen](/program/ZKJQAP/), [Larene Le Gassick](/program/DVTWWU/), and other talks from [our program](/program/list/) are available on the [PyCon AU YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/user/PyConAU).

Thank you to everyone who made PyCon AU 2025 an amazing event by volunteering, sponsoring, speaking, or attending. We couldn't have done this without YOU.

Find the latest PyCon AU news at https://pycon.org.au/, by following us on:

- [Mastodon](https://mastodon.pycon.org.au/@pyconau),
- [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/pyconau/),
